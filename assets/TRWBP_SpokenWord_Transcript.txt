Adam Vials Moore
November 2025

You will not be able to access the research, brothers and sisters,
Because your library cancelled the subscription.

You did the research with your own hands,
You reviewed the papers for free,
You edited the journal for nothing,
And then they charged you thousands to publish,
Millions to read it back.

This isn't a publishing model.
This is extraction.
This is capitalism in a white coat.
