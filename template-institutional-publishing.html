Examples of Institutional Publishing Programs

  • eLife - Research organization-supported Diamond OA journal in life sciences
  • Stanford University Press - Hosts multiple open access journals and books
  • Michigan Publishing - University of Michigan's comprehensive publishing program
  • Ubiquity Press - University partnerships for hosting OA journals

Key Technology: Open Journal Systems (OJS)

OJS is free, open-source software developed by the Public Knowledge Project. It powers over 10,000 journals worldwide and includes:

  • Full peer review workflow management
  • Author and reader interfaces
  • Indexing and discovery integration
  • Plugin architecture for customization
