What is DORA?

The Declaration on Research Assessment (DORA) recognizes the need to improve the ways in which researchers and research outputs are evaluated. Key principles:

  • Do not use journal-based metrics as a surrogate measure of individual article quality
  • Assess research on its own merits rather than on the journal where published
  • Eliminate the use of journal impact factors in hiring, promotion, and funding decisions
  • Consider the value and impact of all research outputs, not just publications

Over 20,000 organizations and individuals have signed DORA, including major funders and research institutions worldwide.
