Building an Effective Working Group

Do's:

  • Start small: Monthly coffee meetings are more sustainable than ambitious programs
  • Be educational: Focus on learning together, not preaching to each other
  • Share resources: Build a shared list of Diamond OA journals in your fields
  • Invite experts: Library OA specialists, scholarly communication librarians
  • Document: Keep notes and create resources others can use

Don'ts:

  • Don't judge: Career pressures are real. Support choices, don't criticize them.
  • Don't overcommit: Grad students are busy. Keep it manageable.
  • Don't get dogmatic: There's room for nuance in publication decisions.

Potential Activities

  • Journal club discussing articles on scholarly publishing
  • Workshops on copyright, preprints, and publisher agreements
  • Guest speakers from Diamond OA journals in your field
  • Creating a "publishing guide" for your department
