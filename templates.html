Template Letters

Ready-to-customize letter templates for taking action in your role. Each template includes guidance on when to use it, customization notes, and specific language you can adapt.

How to use these templates:

  • Click any template to view it in full
  • Customize the [BRACKETED] placeholders with your specific information
  • Add institution-specific data and examples
  • Adjust tone to match your relationship with the recipient
